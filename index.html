About Tagisan ng Sining - STI College Marikina

Tagisan ng Sining is our annual competition for STIers to showcase their artistic talents. This is the official local entry for STI College Marikina, where the winners will represent our campus in the national competition. It's a celebration of creativity, identity, and the Filipino spirit through various forms of art.
